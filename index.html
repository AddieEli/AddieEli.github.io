Apiary App

As a beekeeper, I wanted to design an app that would allow a beekeeper to easily track hive status, available food sources, and external/internal temperature. I want to create tools that help beekeepers collect and share data in the effort of stopping CCD. The app uses Ruby on Rails, Javascript, and C++ for a thermometer set into my personal hive.
